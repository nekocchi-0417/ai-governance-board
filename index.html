AI Governance Ecosystem (working prototype)

Everyone here has more power than you'd think — and is more boxed in than they'd admit.

The race to build advanced AI isn't one story — it's many actors pulling with and against each other, each chasing what it wants and hemmed in by what limits it. That gap between ambition and constraint is where governance actually happens. Pick an actor, or trace a tension between two.

Tap an actor to open its card · tap a line to read the tension · flip the lens to surface constraints

This is a working prototype, one of my explorations on visual explainers for AI governance. The ecosystem map here (who each actor is, wants, fears, and what limits them) took reference from (1) bluedot's AGI Strategy: Character Cards and (2) the IAPP report on Mapping and understanding the AI governance ecosystem.
